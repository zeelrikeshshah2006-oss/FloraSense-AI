About

FloraSense AI is a flower species recognition and disease detection system designed to identify different types of flowers and detect possible diseases in them. It uses advanced Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning techniques to analyze flower images and provide accurate predictions. The system helps users quickly understand the flower type and its health condition, making plant care easier and smarter.
